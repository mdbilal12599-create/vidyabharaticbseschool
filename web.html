VISION

Inspire greatness in each and every child. Developing and delivering a quality education system. Address the aspirations and abilities of our students. Develop honest citizens. Nurture and harness leadership ability among students.

OUR MISSION

Vidya Bharti CBSE School recognizes that each child is an individual. We believe all children are creative and need to succeed. Therefore, our school respects the individual needs of children, fosters a caring and creative environment, and emphasizes the social, emotional, physical, and intellectual development of every child.

INTRODUCTION

Vidya Bharati Shikshana Kendra was established to meet the ever-growing need for quality education and holistic development of students. Founded in 1980, the institution has grown into a well-developed center of learning with modern infrastructure and facilities. Today, the school has more than 1000 students and over 85 dedicated staff members. Montessori education was introduced in 2012, and the CBSE curriculum was adopted in 2014.
